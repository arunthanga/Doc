# Function / Marriage Hall — Alternate-Use Layout (private planning note)

> **Private planning document — keep separate from the biochar DPR and out of any DIC/bank submission.**
> This shows how the **same building shell** (the ~9,000 sq ft single clear-span hall on the 38-cent corner plot, roads on south & east) can serve as a **function / marriage hall**. It reuses the *fixed* structures of the biochar unit so a future changeover is minimal:
>
> | Biochar (as-built, fixed) | Function-hall use |
> |---|---|
> | North process & utilities stretch (power, water, plumbing, stack) | **Kitchen + service + power** (north, fixed) |
> | West rooms (lab, QC, office, secure store) | **Rooms behind the stage** — bride, groom, relatives |
> | North-west toilet / handwash block | **Guest toilets & handwash** (shared) |
> | Large open working floor + movable partition | **Function hall + dining**, split by the movable partition |

---

## 1. Orientation & Vastu basis

Plot is a near-square **corner plot with roads on the south and east**. Guests enter from the **east** (auspicious); the **stage runs along the west**, so the **couple on stage faces east**.

| Element | Placement here | Vastu note |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage / dais** | **West, full stretch** (except the kitchen/NW portion) | ✔ Stage in West → couple faces **East** |
| **Main entrance** | **East** (from east road) | ✔ East entry is ideal |
| **Bride / groom rooms** | **West, behind the stage backdrop** | ✔ Couple/green rooms toward West / SW |
| **Toilets & handwash** | **North-west** | ✔ Toilets in NW/West |
| **Water / borewell** | **North-east** | ✔ Water in NE |
| **Kitchen + power/service** | **North, full stretch (fixed)** | Practical (all power/plumbing/sewage on N & W). *Vastu ideal for cooking is SE; placed north here by choice for services — acceptable trade-off.* |
| **Dining** | **Centre/east of the hall**, beside the function area | Served from the north kitchen |

---

## 2. Zoning (single ~9,000 sq ft hall)

- **North stretch — Kitchen + service + power (fixed, ~15–20%, ~1,500 sq ft).** Cooking, prep, wash-up, service counters opening to the dining side; exhaust/chimney at the north; the electrical board and plumbing/sewage run along the **north and west**. **No partition to the hall is required now — it can be added later.**
- **West strip — Stage + rooms.** The **stage** runs the full west length (except the kitchen/NW). **Behind the stage backdrop:** a **bride's room**, a **groom's room** and **1–2 relatives'/green rooms** (these are the biochar unit's lab/office/store rooms). **Toilets & handwash** at the **north-west**.
- **Main hall — Function/Marriage hall + Dining (as large as possible).** One large clear-span space in front of the stage, divided by a **single movable partition**:
  - **Function/marriage side (larger)** — seating faces the west stage.
  - **Dining side (smaller, ~¼ of the gathering)** — when the ceremony ends, **open/roll back the movable partition to enlarge the dining area**.

---

## 3. Indicative capacity

- **Function/marriage hall:** ~600 guests (theatre/loose seating), stage on the west.
- **Dining:** seated for **~¼ of the gathering (~150)** at a time; **expandable** by opening the movable partition after the ceremony.
- Two service flows via the corner plot: **guests from the east gate**, **kitchen/service from the south gate**.

---

## 4. Fixtures & services checklist

- **Kitchen (north):** 3-phase power, gas bank, water supply + drainage, wash-up, cold store, service counters, exhaust hood + chimney.
- **Handwash:** counters at the **dining–kitchen edge** (multiple basins) + at the toilet block.
- **Toilets (NW, shared, fixed):** separate **ladies** and **gents** blocks, each with multiple WCs + wash basins; usable by both the hall and the biochar unit.
- **Stage (west):** raised dais + backdrop; power for lighting/AV; access to the rooms behind.
- **Rooms behind stage:** bride room + groom room (attached wash where possible) + relatives'/green room.
- **Dining:** open floor beside the function hall; buffet counters fed from the north kitchen.
- **Movable partition:** one sliding/folding partition between the function and dining zones (the only movable divider; everything else is fixed).
